Giuseppe Sulfaro

Giuseppe Sulfaro was born on October 7th 1984 in Messina, Sicily. He starred in Malèna as Renato at the age of 14 having never acted before. He was among 2500 hopefuls who sent Guiseppe Tornatore photos as the process to cast Monica Bellucci's co-star began. Of these, 90 were chosen for audition, 40 made it through to the testing stage and 3 finalists had their videos sent to Miramax for approval. Sulfaro was catipulted into the world of the cinema, taking a year of school. While shooting took place he had to manage his studies around his filming schedule.
Since Malèna Guiseppe has appeared in mainly Italian films and television shows. He also appeared in the Greek Language movie "A hero.......In Rome" which meant a three month crash course in learning Greek.
