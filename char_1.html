Malèna

Malèna, has been left in her husband's village while he fights for the Italian army. Mussolini has declared war on England and France. Each day she walks through the village, and it sends the otherwise bored inhabitants into a curiosity frenzy, idle gossip soon turns to vicious rumour and her character has been damned by the women folk and lustful fantasies fill the minds of the men young and old. The results are both moving and ridiculous. The rumours spread as far as her father, one of the local school teachers, he cuts her off and when news of her husband missing presumed dead comes through our heroine descends into a spiral of grief and a course of self preservation or self destruction.
