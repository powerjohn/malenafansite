News

Monica Bellucci:Monica Bellucci is currently working on two movies: "On The Milky Way" with Serbian Director and two-time winner of the palm d'Or Emir Kusturica which is being filmed in Bosnia and "Le Meraviglie" with novice Italian director Alice Rohrwacher which is being filmed in Tuscany, Italy. Both films are due to be released in 2014.

Giuseppe Tornatore: Tornatore's latest film, "The Best Offer" was released in September. It is his first attempt at an English language film, and introduces the Dutch actress,Sylvia Hoeks who is also making her debut in an English Language movie. It was filmed in over half a dozen European cities as the director tried to create a setting that could not be defined as one particular place.

Ennio Morricone: Ennio Morricone is currently touring with an ensemble of 200 musicians and singers with dates in November in Chile. Early next year he will be appearing in France and Bulgaria and returns to New York and Los Angeles in March.
