The Plot

The movie opens on a momentous day in the life of the 13 year old Renato He gets his first bike and his first glimpse of the most beautiful woman in the vilage, Malèna. A woman who is the subject of lust from the male population of the village and jealousy and spite from the town's female population. She finds herself as a suspicious stranger in her husband's village, left behind as he is stationed overseas with the Italian army during the second world war. A victim of gossip and circumstance, the story unfolds and news reaches her of the death of Nino, her husband. What will now become of her? Will she become the loose woman the village has her portrayed as or can Renato save her from her fate?......
