Monica Bellucci

Monica Bellucci was born on September 30th 1964 in Umbria, Italy. She began modelling for local amatuer photographers from the age of 13. In 1988 she moved to the Italian capital of fashion, Milan and soon signed with the famous Elite Modelling agency.
In the early 90's she had some small roles in film's like "Bram Stokers Dracula". Her nomination for a Cesar Award as best supporting actress in "The Apartment" in 1996 brought her to the attention of critics and audiences alike.
In 2000 she was nominated at the European Film Awards for the audience Award for best actress for her portayal of Malèna. Her role, Persephone in the Matrix trilogy brought her to the attention of American audiences.
She continues to work in Europe and America.
