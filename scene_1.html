The Court Room

The wife of the local dentist takes Malèna to court. Malèna is accused of having an affair with her husband after he had been found fighting with a German Officer outside Malèna's house. The deluded dentist had accused the soldier of secret meetings with his fiancée (Malèna). Malèna is acquitted. The court is told that Malena is being harassed for being beautiful as other ladies feel insecure and threatened by her. After her acquittal, Malena's lawyer comes to her home and asks for dance using her unpaid legal fee as leverage, he forces himself upon her while Renato looks on from outside her house.
